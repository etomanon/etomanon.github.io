Analysis of open geospatial data and the concept of its application

This bachelorâ€™s thesis studies issues of open spatial data. It describes definition of open data and its possible geographic formats. It examines possibilities of sharing spatial data and covers main sources of open spatial data in the Czech Republic. It also analyses chosen examples of commercial use of open spatial data abroad. Finally, it suggests own prototypic application in the area of traffic data.

I have used 3 datasets to cover the studied region of D2 highway - Police of the Czech Republic, RODOS and OpenStreetMap. I have formed a data model and programmed scripts to get, transform and insert data into PostgreSQL database. After that, I have connected the database to GeoServer. Thanks to that, I have created a web map. The thesis also lists shortcomings of current traffic maps and proposes possible solutions.
